Man-style macaroni
From: Aunt Nora

2 c or 8 oz macaroni
1/4 c onion, chopped 
1/4 c butter
1/4 c flour
2 c milk
1/8 t cayenne pepper
1/2 lb (2 c) sharp cheese, grated
8 wieners, sliced
1/2 t salt

cook macaroni in boiling salted water until tender. strain.
Cook onions in butter until tender.  Blend in flour, gradually add milk.  Cook until smooth and thick, stir constantly.
Add salt, pepper, and cheese.Combine macaroni, wieners, & cheeses sauce.  Put into baking dish.  Sprinkle paprika over top.  Bake in 350 oven for 25 minutes or until bubbling brown.  

Serves 6-8 people
