Vegan Pumpkin Oatmeal Cookies

1 cup canned or cooked pumpkin
1 cup brown sugar
½ cup applesauce, mixed with ½ t. baking powder (egg replacement)
1 ½ cups whole wheat flour
1 t. baking soda
1 t. cinnamon
½ t. nutmeg
½ t. cloves
3 cups oatmeal
1 cup raisins
1/3 cup soymilk

Thoroughly mix pumpkin and brown sugar; then beat in egg substitute.  Gradually mix in flour, soda, and spices, then mix in oatmeal and raisins.  Add soymilk as necessary for proper consistency.  Drop by spoonfuls on non-stick or oil-sprayed cookie sheets.  Bake at 350º for 10-12 minutes.
Makes 4-5 dozen.
